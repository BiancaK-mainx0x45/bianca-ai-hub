Elevator Pitch

Your 30-Second Intro

Finance professional transitioning into AI/Technology. Currently building AI literacy programs and products that make AI adoption frictionless for teams. Differentiators: responsible AI governance, the human element of adoption, and designing systems that free people from busywork to focus on bigger thinking.

Why I'm Transitioning (60s)

I spent years in finance optimizing processes and managing large-scale programs. But I realized the real impact comes from enabling humans to do better work. That's why I'm moving into AI adoption and enablement. It's about helping teams and organizations see AI not as a threat, but as a way to eliminate the work that drains them. I want to be the bridge who maps tribal knowledge across an organization so AI adoption is frictionless.

Product Thinking (Headspace Focus)

What excites me about Headspace is the mission to make mental health tools accessible at scale. I'm interested in how AI can expand reach without diluting quality. At my last role, I scaled Salesforce adoption from 30% to 95% by focusing on the human-centered adoption part, not just the tool. I think that approach applies here — using AI to handle the repetitive parts of member onboarding and support so your team can focus on the insight and care.
