The Short Version

Seven years in finance — model governance, stress testing, regulatory reporting. The core question was always: when this model is wrong, who bears the cost?

That question travels perfectly into AI. In 2026 I shifted focus: not just to what AI can do, but to what it gets wrong — and who builds the bridge between the technology and the humans who need to use it responsibly. That bridge is the job I'm building toward.

Everything in this portfolio was built during a self-directed 18-week AI curriculum. No bootcamp. No cohort. A structured roadmap, a bias toward building over watching, and a finance professional's instinct to verify before trusting.
