🏋️ Post-Workout Skin Protocol
FitSmart says you worked out today! Here's how to protect your skin:
1. Cleanse within 30 min — sweat left on skin clogs pores and worsens hormone-related breakouts
2. Use a gentle, fragrance-free cleanser — avoid harsh formulas after exercise
3. Reapply SPF if heading outdoors after your session
4. A hyaluronic acid serum after cleansing helps restore hydration lost through sweat
5. If hormone-friendly mode is on — avoid heat-generating products (peels, strong retinol) on workout days
